About

Student-led blockchain research, education, and innovation at Emory University. Building the future of decentralized technology through academic excellence and practical implementation.

Mission & Vision

Advancing blockchain technology through education, research, and innovation

Our Mission

Our mission is to bridge the gap between the complex fintech industry and our members by fostering a deeper understanding of blockchain. Through engaging lesson plans, talks with industry experts, and networking events, we equip our members with the knowledge and skills necessary to navigate the ever-evolving landscape of the blockchain industry.

The interest group is a dynamic platform for students passionate about blockchain, providing valuable insights and preparing them for future opportunities in this transformative space.
