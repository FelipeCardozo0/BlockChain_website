Meet the Team

Directors

Our leadership team drives strategic vision and oversees all club operations. They ensure we maintain our mission of empowering students in blockchain innovation while building strong industry partnerships and academic connections.

Hackers

Our technical experts focus on hands-on development, smart contract creation, and blockchain infrastructure. They lead workshops, hackathons, and technical projects that bridge theory with real-world applications.

Researchers

Our research team explores cutting-edge blockchain technologies, publishes academic papers, and conducts market analysis. They stay ahead of industry trends and contribute to the broader blockchain ecosystem.

Our investment team manages portfolio strategies, conducts due diligence, and identifies emerging opportunities in the blockchain space. They provide financial education and real-world investment experience to our members.
